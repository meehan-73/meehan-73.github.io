Where I Spend My Spare Time

When I'm not working on projects that have my interest, I'm usually spending time with my wife (Amy) and dog (Wes). Amy and I met at Notre Dame, and we've been happy together ever since. We are HUGE Notre Dame fans. A few years in, we rescued our Australian Cattle Dog / Shiba Inu mix, Wes, pictured below.

I'm also usually trying to stay physically active (healthy body --> healthy mind), usually in the form of powerlifting. Powerlifting is a sport where the highest weight moved in each of three lifts (squat, bench, deadlift) is added up to compose a powerlifter's 'total." The highest total in each weight class wins. Currently, my all-time maxes are a 405lb squat, a 340lb bench, and a 520lb deadlift (1265lb total) at about 217lbs bodyweight. My love of powerlifting is compelled by my love of data; tracking quantifiable key metrics over the course of a training program, like total volume, intensity, and stress can be incredibly signficant in getting stronger and "working smarter not harder."

Fantasy football is another hobby fueled by my inner data nerd. You're telling me that, as someone who loves sports in general, I also get the opportunity to try to predict which specific players are going to perform above/below specific expectations? Sold. At the very least, playing fantasy gives me the (very fun) opportunity to annoy my leaguemates with my statistical musings year in and year out.

I've also been having an absolute blast with my Nintendo Switch. Some favorites of mine are Super Mario Maker 2, Fortnite, and the Jackbox Party Pack Collection.
